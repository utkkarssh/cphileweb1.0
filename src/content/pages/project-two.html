Distribution Opportunities

Franchise-Based Dealership & Distributorship with Chipshophile Private Limited

At Chipshophile Private Limited, we’re not just creating snacks — we’re creating opportunities. With a fast-growing product line, strong brand appeal, and a robust supply chain, we invite you to partner with us as a franchisee and become a key part of India’s snack revolution.

Our franchise-based distribution model allows passionate entrepreneurs, FMCG professionals, and retail investors to tap into the booming snacks segment by choosing a dealership or distribution role based on market size, investment capacity, and operational capabilities.

Opportunities Available

Super Stockist / Dealership

Ideal for regional players with warehouse and logistics capacity, our super stockist partners are responsible for:

  • Procuring products in bulk directly from Chipshophile
  • Managing regional inventory
  • Supplying to multiple distributors or sub-distributors
  • Overseeing sales operations within assigned zones

Distributor / Sub-Distributor

Our distributors are the heartbeat of our local market presence. They:

  • Handle supply to retailers, wholesalers, and modern trade outlets
  • Promote Chipshophile’s presence in their locality or district
  • Receive strong sales and marketing support from our team
  • Work in collaboration with super stockists and the central sales team

This tier is ideal for individuals or businesses looking for lower investment but high-return distribution models.

Why Partner with Chipshophile?

  • Wide Product Range – Chips, namkeens, fryums, and private label offerings
  • Attractive Margins – Competitive dealer and distributor margins
  • Established Branding – Professionally designed packs with strong shelf appeal
  • Marketing Support – POS materials, digital promotion, sampling campaigns
  • Training & Guidance – Product knowledge, territory planning, and launch support
  • Scalable Business Model – Start local, scale regionally or nationally

Logistics & Supply Chain

We offer fast and reliable delivery from our manufacturing unit in Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh, directly to your warehouse or local stock point. Our operations team works closely with franchise partners to ensure consistent availability and prompt restocking.

Terms of Engagement

  • A Letter of Intent (LOI) is required to initiate dealership or distributorship onboarding.
  • We offer flexible supply volumes based on your operational scale.
  • Initial investment and order volume vary by region and product categories.
  • All partners receive standard business documentation including invoices, delivery challans, and GST-compliant billing.
