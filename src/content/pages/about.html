The Taste Behind Every Crunch

At Chipshophile Private Limited, we’re not just making snacks — we’re shaping experiences. Established in the heart of Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh, our company was founded with a bold mission: to deliver superior-quality snacks that combine great taste, authentic Indian flavors, and uncompromising food safety standards.

We specialize in the manufacturing of fryums, chips, namkeens, and other innovative Indian snack varieties that appeal to all age groups. With growing demand in both domestic and international markets, Chipshophile has positioned itself as a rising player in the Indian FMCG landscape, blending modern manufacturing processes with a deep respect for local snacking culture.

Our Commitment

What sets Chipshophile apart is our focus on consistency, hygiene, packaging, and customer satisfaction. We aim to deliver value-for-money products without sacrificing taste or quality. Our plant is designed to produce at scale while maintaining strict quality checks and food-grade safety controls at every stage.

We are also committed to sustainability, ethical sourcing, and efficient logistics, enabling us to provide freshness and flavor that travel far and last longer — from small-town kirana stores to global retail chains.

Why Chipshophile

  • Professional team with deep roots in the FMCG and snacks industry
  • Strong production capacity tailored for domestic and export requirements
  • In-house branding and packaging design capabilities
  • Quick adaptation to evolving consumer trends and regional tastes
  • Transparent business practices with competitive pricing
  • Ability to serve a wide variety of B2B and retail formats

Trusted Partnerships

Our growing list of distribution partners, export clients, and institutional buyers reflects the trust we’ve built through consistent delivery and dependable service. We understand regional market dynamics and are equipped to meet the volume and quality expectations of partners across India and abroad.

Vision

To become a globally recognized Indian snack brand, built on the pillars of taste, trust, and transparency, while offering sustainable opportunities for partners, employees, and communities.

Mission

To deliver safe, flavorful, and affordable snacks that celebrate Indian culinary diversity, while continuously innovating in product design, packaging, and distribution models.

Chipshophile Private Limited is not just a snack company. We are your next favorite taste, your next business partner, and your trusted name in every bite.
