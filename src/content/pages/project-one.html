Exports – Chipshophile Private Limited

At Chipshophile Private Limited, we bring India’s rich snacking heritage to global markets through our meticulously crafted range of snacks. With a commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we offer seamless export solutions for buyers and distributors around the world. Our products are crafted to meet international standards, ensuring excellence in both taste and compliance.

Our Export Product Range

  • Potato Chips (Classic Salted, Masala, Peri Peri, Cream & Onion, etc.)
  • Namkeens (Mixtures, Bhujia, Masala Sev, Moong Dal, Aloo Bhujia)
  • Fryums (Wheel Fryums, Pipe Fryums, Star Fryums, Plain Fryums)
  • Customizable Snack Solutions for private labeling (flavors, packaging, branding)

All products are available for export in bulk packs, consumer-ready retail packs, or as OEM private label orders based on buyer requirements.

Global Quality & Compliance

Our snacks are manufactured under stringent hygiene conditions and comply with global food safety and export norms. We use premium-grade ingredients and follow internationally recognized manufacturing practices, ensuring consistent taste, texture, and shelf life. From raw material sourcing to final dispatch, our processes are designed to meet the regulatory standards of different countries and continents.

Incoterms Offered

  • FOB (Free On Board) – Shipment delivered to the port of departure.
  • CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight) – We handle shipping and insurance up to the destination port.

We work closely with freight forwarders and shipping agents to ensure safe, timely, and efficient deliveries.

Payment Terms

To ensure transparency and trust, we offer secure and flexible international payment structures:

  1. 50% Advance + 50% Before Dispatch
  2. 50% Advance + 50% via Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC)

A Letter of Intent (LOI) from the buyer is required to initiate the export process, confirming their interest, quantity, destination, and agreed-upon terms.

Export Documentation & Certifications

We provide all necessary documentation and certifications required for smooth international customs clearance and compliance:

  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing List
  • Bill of Lading
  • Shipping Bill
  • Insurance Certificate
  • Phytosanitary Certificate
  • Inspection Certificate
  • Health Certificate

Additional certificates or third-party inspection can be arranged based on buyer or country-specific import requirements.

Shipment & Volume

We offer flexible shipment volumes to suit both wholesale and large-scale distribution:

  • Container Shipments (20ft / 40ft containers)
  • Bulk Orders in Metric Tons (MT)

Packaging options can be tailored for retail shelves, wholesale supply chains, or private-label packaging as per buyer preferences.

At Chipshophile, we are committed to building long-term international partnerships through professionalism, product excellence, and reliable fulfillment. Let us be your trusted Indian snacking partner in global trade.
